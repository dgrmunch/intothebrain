PRELIMINARY WORK

Proprioception & Self-Awareness in VR Environments

We have been exploring the potential of VR environments in the development of psychosocial interventions, such as the use of the virtual worlds to enhance proprioception and self-awareness in young adults, or avatar-mediated spaces for teenagers dealing with anxiety. 

Immersive spaces for promoting well-being and mindfulness

We are studying how Immersive Physical Spaces, Virtual Reality and Sensory Stimulation can enable innovative mindfulness-based interventions with a focus on mental well-being.
